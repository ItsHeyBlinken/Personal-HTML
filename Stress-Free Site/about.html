Our Story at Stress Free Events & Weddings

At Stress Free Events & Weddings, our ultimate goal is to see the radiant smiles on our couples' faces. With our dedicated team, we strive to surpass your expectations and transform your dream wedding into a beautiful reality. As part of the Stress Free family, your special day will unfold seamlessly, allowing you to cherish every moment with your loved ones without the burden of worrying about details.


Serving Dallas and the surrounding DFW areas, our skilled coordinators will be by your side throughout your engagement, attentively listening to your vision to ensure your wedding day is nothing short of extraordinary.

Your Dream Team

Lisa - Owner

Lisa, the proprietor and creator of Stress Free Events & Weddings, has been bringing couples' dream weddings to life for more than two decades. While juggling her role as an auditor in Corporate America, she established Stress Free in 2016. Two years later, Lisa bid farewell to her auditing career, recognizing her true passion lay in orchestrating every aspect of couples' special days, from venue selection to exchanging vows with their beloved.



Natalie - Operations Director

As a proud member of the Stress Free Events & Weddings team, I find immense joy in witnessing the happiness on our couples' faces. With a passion for meticulous planning and a dedication to excellence, I strive to turn your wedding dreams into a flawless reality. Serving as your trusted coordinator, my priority is to ensure that your special day unfolds seamlessly, allowing you to savor every moment with your loved ones.
